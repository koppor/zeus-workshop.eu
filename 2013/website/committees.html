Services and their Composition

5th Central European Workshop on Services and their Composition, ZEUS 2013
February 21-22, 2013, Rostock, Germany

Join a different workshop!

ZEUS focuses on the discussion of fresh ideas, the presentation of work in progress, and the establishment of a scientific network of young researchers in the region.
