Services and their Composition

5th Central European Workshop on Services and their Composition, ZEUS 2013
February 21-22, 2013, Rostock, Germany

Join a different workshop!

ZEUS focuses on the discussion of fresh ideas, the presentation of work in progress, and the establishment of a scientific network of young researchers in the region.

CEUR-WS.org Post-Proceedings

The post-proceedings will be published in the series CEUR Workshop Proceedings.

Venue and Accommodation

Venue

The workshop shall take place in the new building of the Computer Science Department of the University of Rostock. The street address is

Konrad-Zuse-Haus
Albert-Einstein-Straße 22
18059 Rostock
and can be conveniently reached by public transport (tram line 6 to station Mensa) or car (parking space available).

Close to the venue is the Südmensa cafeteria offering awarded, yet affordable lunches.

Workshop Dinner

We shall have a workshop dinner on Thursday evening, beginning at 20:00 at the Restaurant Lenk's at the city harbor. It's easiest to take the tram line 6 and leave at "Doberaner Platz" or "Kabutzenhof". From there, walk down to the city harbor.

Alternativeley, you can meet us at 18:30 at the fountain of the city market ("Neuer Markt", also reachable by tram line 6) and we walk together through the center of Rostock, down to the city harbor and to the restaurant. So if you need to bring your luggage to your hotel, you can decide to meet us at the market or directly at the restaurant.
